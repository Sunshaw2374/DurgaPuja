ॐ Durga Puja ॐ

Durga Puja, also called Durgotsava, is an annual Hindu festival originating in the Indian subcontinent which reveres and pays homage to the Hindu goddess, Durga. It is widely celebrated not only India, but also in the whole world in a huge excitement. Many beautiful pandals and idols are made to enjoy this festival anually. So, in this HTML Document we will see some dates related to this Puja and some of the popular places in Kolkata which are known for their extraordinary and unique themes in this auspicious festival.

Date(Tithi) Date(Calendar) Day
MahaShasti 22 October Thursday
MahaSaptami 23 October Friday
MahaAshtami 24 October Saturday
MahaNavami 25 October Sunday
VijoyaDashami 26 October Monday

1. SreeBhumi Sporting Club_2017
Theme:- Mahismati Palace, Bahubali
Considered one of the best pujas in Kolkata, SreeBhumi Sporting Club set its theme as the Mahismati Palace Of Bahubali and attracted many people.

2.Santosh Mitra Square(2019)
Theme:-Seeshmahal,Jaipur
Featuring a famous fort of Jaipur (Seeshamahal) as its pandal, the idol of Goddess Durga was made up of 20 Crore Gold which attracted people from around the world not wanting to miss to see it.

3.College Square(2019)
Theme:- Swami Narayan Mandir of Gujarat
One of the oldest Durga Puja committees of Kolkata, College Square has its unique theme everytime,with a great sense of enthusiasm every year. The theme of the puja was Sabeki.

________________________________________________________________________________________

4.Bagbazar Sarbojanin(2018)
Years completed(till 2018):- 100
The main attraction of this pandal is the traditional deity. Sindoor Khela is also popular. This pandal is surrounded by carnival rides, stalls and others, which attract millions of people.

________________________________________________________________________________________

5.Suruchi Sangha(2019)
Theme for every year:- A state of the country.
This Puja of Kolkata is also more than 50 years old. The Pandal is located in New Alipore of South Kolkata. It has also won awards for being the best-decorated pandal 2 times, once in 2003 and again in 2009.

________________________________________________________________________________________

6.Deshapriya Park(2015)
Speciality:- Tallest Idol of Goddess Durga(88 feet high and 80 feet wide)
Not only in West Bengal, not only in India, but this was the world's Tallest Idol of Goddess Durga. Thousands of people were coming to see this puja which was fantastic, brilliant and beautiful.

________________________________________________________________________________________

7.EkDalia Evergreen Club(2019)
Theme:-Jatoli Shiv Temple, Shimla, Himachal Pradesh
Though simple, but lakhs of people visit every year to Ekdalia Puja. The Club creates replicas of Temples from all over India and of World Heritage Sites.

________________________________________________________________________________________

8.2016_Dum Dum Park
Years completed in 2016:- 50
One of the beautiful and elegant pujas in Kolkata, Dum Dum Park creates very unique pandals and pujas with different designs which are wonderful and eye-catching.

________________________________________________________________________________________

9. Kumartuli Park(2019)
Theme:-Space(Science) and Intergalactic Connections.
With an elaborate make-shift rocket launcher at the front of the pandal, this puja is very innovative and exciting to see. Grand Puja is made by this association every year.

________________________________________________________________________________________

10.Chetla Agrani(2019)
Age of Puja(as of 2019)-27
With very beautiful pandals and idols, Chetla Agrani has rows of people standing in queue to see this marvellous Puja. Its beautiful themes and amazing pandals with beautiful idols make it one of the best pujas in Kolkata.
